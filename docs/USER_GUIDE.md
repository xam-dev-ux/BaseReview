# BaseReview User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [basereview.xyz](https://basereview.xyz)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
3. Select MetaMask (or your preferred wallet)
4. Approve the connection
5. Ensure you're on Base Mainnet (the site will prompt you to switch if not)

### 2. Browse Apps

#### Search
- Use the search bar to find apps by name or URL
- Autocomplete suggests matching apps as you type

#### Filter
- **Category**: DeFi, Gaming, NFT, Social, Utility, DAO, Other
- **Verification Status**: Official, Developer Verified, Community Verified, Unverified, Flagged, Scam
- **Rating**: Filter by minimum/maximum stars
- **Tags**: Filter by positive/negative/scam tags

#### Sort
- **Highest Rated**: Best reviewed apps first
- **Most Reviewed**: Most popular apps
- **Recently Added**: Newest apps on platform
- **Trending**: Most activity this week

### 4. Writing Reviews

#### Requirements
- Wallet connected to Base network
- Minimum stake: 0.0001 ETH (refunded if review gets 10+ helpful votes)
- Account at least 7 days old
- Haven't already reviewed this app

#### Steps
1. Navigate to the app's detail page
2. Click "Write a Review"
3. Rate the app (1-5 stars)
4. Select review type (General, Warning, Scam Report, Positive)
5. Add tags describing your experience
6. Write detailed review (minimum 50 characters)
7. Optional: Add proof (screenshots, transaction hashes)
8. Confirm and submit (pays stake + gas)

- âœ… Be specific and detailed

If reporting a scam, you **must** provide:

- Proof: Screenshots and/or transaction hashes
- Detailed explanation of what happened

False scam reports result in reputation penalties.

### 7. Scam Protection

#### Warning Signs
- New app with no reviews
- Unrealistic promises ("10,000% APY!")
- Cannot withdraw funds
- Developer anonymous with no verification
- Multiple scam reports
- Website/contract has honeypot patterns

#### If You Encounter a Scam
1. **Do NOT interact further** with the app
2. **File a scam report** immediately
3. **Provide evidence**: Screenshots, tx hashes
4. **Warn others**: Share on social media
5. **Report to Base**: Notify Base team if serious

#### Protection Features
- Scam report threshold system
- Community voting on disputes
- Developer stake slashing for confirmed scams
- Real-time warnings on flagged apps

### 8. Managing Your Profile

#### View Your Stats
- Total reviews written
- Reputation score (0-100)
- Helpful votes received
- Successful scam reports
- Badges earned

#### Edit Reviews
- Edit within 24 hours of posting
- After 24h, must delete and re-review (30 day cooldown)
- Edited reviews marked with timestamp

#### Delete Reviews
- Can delete own reviews anytime
- Forfeit your stake when deleting
- Resets your review status for that app

### 9. Reputation Building

#### How to Earn Reputation
1. **Write quality reviews**: Detailed, helpful content
2. **Get helpful votes**: Others find your reviews useful
3. **Age your account**: Older wallets = more trust
4. **File valid scam reports**: Help protect community
5. **Align with consensus**: Match community sentiment

#### Reputation Benefits
- Higher vote weight (up to 2x at 80+)
- Verified badge at 50+
- More influence in disputes
- Community trust and recognition

#### Penalties
- Lost disputes: -5 points each
- Removed reviews: Reputation hit
- Fake/spam reviews: Severe penalties

### 10. FAQs

**Q: Why do I need to stake to review?**
A: Prevents spam and fake reviews. You get it back if your review is helpful (10+ votes).

**Q: Can developers delete my review?**
A: No. Reviews are stored on blockchain and immutable. Only you or governance can remove.

**Q: What if my review is disputed?**
A: Community votes on disputes. If you provided honest review with proof, you'll likely win.

**Q: How long until my review appears?**
A: Immediately after blockchain confirmation (usually <5 seconds on Base).

**Q: Can I review the same app multiple times?**
A: No, one review per app per address. You can edit within 24h or delete and re-review after 30 days.

**Q: Is my identity revealed?**
A: Only your wallet address is shown. Use ENS for a custom name.

**Q: What if I find a bug?**
A: Report it on our GitHub or Discord. Security bugs get rewards!
